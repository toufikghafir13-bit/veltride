PRODUCTS & QUALITY
What is your purity guarantee? +
All VELTRIDE peptides are ≥98% purity verified by High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC). A Certificate of Analysis (COA) from a third-party lab is included with every order.
How should peptides be stored? +
Lyophilized (freeze-dried) peptides should be stored at −20°C for long-term storage, or 2–8°C (refrigerated) for up to 3 months. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles. Keep away from light and moisture.
Are these peptides for human use? +
No. All products sold by VELTRIDE are strictly for in-vitro research and laboratory use only. They are not intended for human or animal consumption, and are not approved by Health Canada for therapeutic use. You must be 18+ to purchase.
What does each lot number mean? +
Each lot number (e.g. VT-001) corresponds to a specific production batch. This allows you to cross-reference your COA and verify the purity data for the exact batch you received.
Can I request a specific peptide not listed? +
Possibly. Contact us on WhatsApp with the compound name, dose, and quantity you need. We'll advise on availability.
