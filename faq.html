Products & Quality
What is your purity guarantee?
All VELTRIDE research compounds are ≥99% purity verified by High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC). A Certificate of Analysis (COA) from a third-party accredited laboratory is included with every order. You can verify the purity of your specific batch using the lot number on your COA.
How should compounds be stored?
Lyophilized (freeze-dried) compounds should be stored at −20°C for long-term storage, or 2–8°C (refrigerated) for up to 3 months after reconstitution. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles. Keep away from light and moisture. Refer to your specific compound’s COA for storage specifications.
Are these products for human use?
No. All products sold by VELTRIDE Inc. are strictly for in-vitro research and laboratory use only. They are not intended for human or animal consumption, and are not approved by Health Canada for therapeutic, diagnostic, medical, cosmetic, or veterinary use. You must be 18 years of age or older to purchase.
What does each lot number mean?
Each lot number (e.g., VT-BPC) corresponds to a specific production batch. This number links your physical product to its Certificate of Analysis, confirming the purity data applies to your exact sample. If you have questions about a specific batch, contact us with the lot number.
Can I request a compound not listed in the catalogue?
Possibly. Contact us via WhatsApp or email with the compound name, form, and quantity required. We will advise on availability and documentation.
