What is Selank?

Selank is a synthetic heptapeptide analogue of the immunomodulatory peptide tuftsin, developed by the Institute of Molecular Genetics in Russia. It has been approved in Russia for anxiety and asthenic disorders and is among the most extensively researched anxiolytic peptides in the world. Selank primarily acts via the enkephalinase inhibition pathway, increasing enkephalin availability in the brain, which produces anxiolytic and mood-stabilizing effects without sedation or dependency risk in research models.
