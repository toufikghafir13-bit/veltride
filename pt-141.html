What is PT-141?

PT-141 (Bremelanotide) is a synthetic cyclic peptide analogue of alpha-MSH (alpha-melanocyte stimulating hormone) that acts on melanocortin receptors MC3R and MC4R in the central nervous system. Unlike vasodilatory approaches to sexual dysfunction research, PT-141 works via CNS pathways — making it one of the only peptides studied for centrally-mediated sexual arousal in both male and female research models. It was FDA-approved in 2019 as Vyleesi for hypoactive sexual desire disorder in premenopausal women.
