Privacy Policy

VELTRIDE Inc. (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA / BC PIPA).

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number
  • Shipping address: for order fulfilment
  • Payment information: processed and stored exclusively by our payment processor — we never see or store your card details
  • Order history: products requested, amounts, dates
  • Researcher qualification confirmation: confirmation that you are 18+ and a qualified researcher

We may also collect basic technical data (browser type, IP address) through standard web server logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Send order confirmations and shipping notifications
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Verify age and researcher qualification requirements

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Vercel — website hosting. This site is hosted on Vercel. Basic server logs may be retained per Vercel’s privacy policy. See vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

WhatsApp — if you contact us via WhatsApp, messages are subject to Meta’s privacy policy.

4. Age & Researcher Verification

By using this Site and placing an order, you confirm you are at least 18 years of age and a qualified researcher using products solely for in-vitro laboratory purposes. We reserve the right to request verification and to cancel orders where these requirements cannot be confirmed.

5. Data Retention

Order and customer data is retained for a minimum of 7 years as required by Canadian tax law. You may request deletion of non-legally-required data at any time.

6. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

Under Canada’s Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent (subject to legal and contractual restrictions)
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

7. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels. The Site uses no third-party analytics services. Standard session cookies may be used for basic site functionality only.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

9. Contact

Privacy inquiries: support@veltride.ca or WhatsApp +1 (250) 718-9152.
