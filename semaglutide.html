What is Semaglutide?

Semaglutide is a glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) receptor agonist with approximately 94% amino acid homology to human GLP-1. It is an acylated peptide with a fatty acid chain that extends its half-life to approximately 7 days, making it a long-acting compound suitable for sustained metabolic research protocols.

VELTRIDE's Semaglutide is supplied as a lyophilized powder with ≥99% HPLC purity, synthesized to analytical reference standard specifications for in-vitro laboratory research applications.

Research Applications

  • GLP-1 receptor binding kinetics and downstream signaling research
  • Metabolic pathway analysis including insulin secretion and glucagon suppression
  • Gastric motility and appetite regulation mechanism studies
  • Comparative pharmacology with other GLP-1 receptor agonists
  • In-vitro cell culture experiments using GLP-1 receptor-expressing cell lines
