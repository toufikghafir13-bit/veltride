What is Tesamorelin?

Tesamorelin is a synthetic analogue of growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) that is FDA-approved under the brand name Egrifta for the treatment of HIV-associated lipodystrophy. It stimulates the anterior pituitary to produce and release growth hormone by binding to GHRH receptors, resulting in elevated GH and IGF-1 levels and, in clinical studies, significant reductions in visceral adipose tissue. Tesamorelin has a transglutaminase modification at the N-terminus that extends its half-life compared to native GHRH.
