Processing Time

Orders placed before 2:00 PM PST Monday–Friday are processed and dispatched the same business day. Orders placed after 2 PM or on weekends are dispatched the next business day. You will receive a tracking number by email once your order ships.

Cold-Chain Packaging

All peptides are lyophilized (freeze-dried) for stability during transit. Every order is packed with:

  • Insulated mylar liner
  • Food-grade ice pack (stays cold 48–72 hrs)
  • Moisture-control desiccant sachet
  • Certificate of Analysis for each product

Upon receipt, transfer to refrigerator (2–8°C) or freezer (−20°C) immediately.

Discreet Packaging

All orders ship in plain brown or white boxes with no VELTRIDE branding, product names, or any identifiable markings on the exterior. The return address appears as a generic BC business address for your privacy.
