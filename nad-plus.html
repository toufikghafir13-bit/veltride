What is NAD+?

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is an essential coenzyme found in all living cells, playing critical roles in cellular energy metabolism, DNA repair via PARP enzymes, and sirtuin-mediated longevity signaling pathways. VELTRIDE NAD+ is supplied as a lyophilized powder with ≥99% HPLC purity for laboratory research.

Research Applications

  • Cellular energy metabolism and mitochondrial function research
  • PARP-mediated DNA repair mechanism studies
  • Sirtuin activation and longevity pathway research
  • NAD+/NADH ratio assays in cell culture
  • Metabolic aging and cellular senescence research
