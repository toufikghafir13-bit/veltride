Overview

What is BPC-157?

BPC-157 (Body Protective Compound 157) is a synthetic pentadecapeptide composed of 15 amino acids. It is derived from a protein found in human gastric juice and represents one of the most extensively researched cytoprotective compounds available in the research community.

VELTRIDE supplies BPC-157 at ≥99% HPLC purity with a Certificate of Analysis available for every lot, shipped discreetly Canada-wide.
