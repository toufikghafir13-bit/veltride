What Is Retatrutide?

Retatrutide is a next-generation triple agonist acting simultaneously on GIP (glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide), GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1), and glucagon receptors. It represents an advanced analytical reference compound for researchers studying multi-receptor metabolic pathway interactions in in-vitro laboratory models.

VELTRIDE supplies Retatrutide at ≥99% HPLC purity with a Certificate of Analysis available for every lot, shipped discreetly Canada-wide. For in-vitro research use only. Not for human consumption.

Research Applications

Key areas of in-vitro research using Retatrutide as a reference standard include:

  • Triple GIP/GLP-1/glucagon receptor co-activation mechanism studies
  • Multi-receptor agonism and downstream cAMP/PKA signaling pathway research
  • Advanced metabolic pathway modeling and comparative analysis
  • Comparative pharmacodynamic studies with mono and dual agonist reference standards
  • Hepatic glucose production regulation research in cell culture models
  • Energy homeostasis pathway research in vitro
  • Next-generation receptor agonist characterization studies
