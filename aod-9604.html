What is AOD-9604?

AOD-9604 (Anti-Obesity Drug 9604) is a synthetic peptide fragment corresponding to amino acids 176-191 of human growth hormone, modified with a tyrosine at the N-terminus. It was originally developed by Monash University in Australia as a safer alternative to full HGH for obesity research.
