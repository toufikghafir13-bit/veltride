About VELTRIDE

A Trusted Canadian Research Source

VELTRIDE is a proudly Canadian supplier of analytical-grade research compounds, dedicated to serving the scientific community, academic institutions, and independent researchers across Canada.

Every compound in our catalogue undergoes rigorous quality verification through High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Mass Spectrometry (MS), ensuring unmatched purity, identity, and stability with every vial.

All products are supplied strictly for laboratory and analytical reference use. Not for human consumption, veterinary, or medical use.
