What is Ipamorelin?

Ipamorelin is a selective fifth-generation growth hormone releasing peptide (GHRP) that stimulates the pituitary gland to produce natural growth hormone pulses. Unlike older GHRPs such as GHRP-2 or GHRP-6, ipamorelin is highly selective — it triggers GH release without significantly raising cortisol or prolactin levels, making it one of the cleanest GHRPs available for research.
