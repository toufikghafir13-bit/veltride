What is Melanotan-2?

Melanotan-2 (MT-2) is a synthetic analogue of alpha-melanocyte stimulating hormone (alpha-MSH) that acts as a broad melanocortin receptor agonist. It binds to MC1R, MC3R, MC4R, and MC5R receptors, producing effects studied in skin pigmentation, sexual function, appetite regulation, and energy balance research. MT-2 originated from University of Arizona research in the 1980s as part of a program to develop photoprotective skin compounds.
